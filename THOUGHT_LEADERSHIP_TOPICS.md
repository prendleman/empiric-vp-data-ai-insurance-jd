# Thought Leadership Topics

This document outlines key thought leadership topics and presentation outlines for representing data and AI capabilities in the Life & Annuity insurance market through industry events, webinars, and advisory discussions.

## Topic 1: Using AI to Improve Lapse & Surrender Management in Life & Annuity

### Presentation Type
- **Format**: Conference session or webinar (45-60 minutes)
- **Audience**: L&A executives, actuaries, product managers, distribution leaders
- **Venues**: 
  - LIMRA Annual Conference
  - SOA (Society of Actuaries) meetings
  - Insurtech conferences (Insurtech Insights, InsureTech Connect)
  - Industry webinars (Insurance Thought Leadership, Carrier Management)

### Outline

**Introduction (5 min)**
- The challenge: Lapse and surrender rates eroding L&A profitability
- Industry context: $XX billion in lost premium revenue annually
- The opportunity: AI-driven early intervention and retention strategies

**The Problem: Understanding Lapse Drivers (10 min)**
- Traditional approaches: Reactive, not predictive
- Key lapse drivers in L&A:
  - Policyholder life events (job loss, health issues, financial stress)
  - Product performance (surrender value, premium increases)
  - Distribution channel effectiveness (agent-driven vs. direct)
  - Competitive pressure and market conditions
- Data challenges: Fragmented data, limited real-time visibility

**The Solution: AI-Powered Predictive Analytics (15 min)**
- Predictive models for early identification of at-risk policyholders
- Feature engineering: Behavioral patterns, payment history, interaction data
- Model explainability: Why a policyholder is at risk (SHAP/LIME)
- Real-time scoring: Continuous monitoring and risk assessment

**Implementation: From Model to Action (10 min)**
- Customer segmentation by risk and value
- Automated retention campaign triggers
- Personalized intervention strategies
- A/B testing and optimization

**Results & Business Impact (10 min)**
- Case study: 20-30% improvement in retention rates
- ROI: 200-400% return on retention analytics investment
- P&L impact: $3-8M annual premium retention per 10,000 policies
- Lessons learned and best practices

**Q&A (10 min)**

### Key Messages
- "AI enables proactive retention, not reactive response"
- "Every 1% improvement in retention = $X million in premium protection"
- "Explainable AI builds trust and enables action"

## Topic 3: Real-time Fraud Detection Across Life Claims

### Presentation Type
- **Format**: Case study or technical session (30-45 minutes)
- **Audience**: Claims executives, fraud investigators, risk managers, data scientists
- **Venues**:
  - Insurance fraud conferences (IASIU - International Association of Special Investigation Units)
  - Claims management conferences
  - AI/ML in insurance events
  - Industry webinars on fraud prevention

### Outline

**Introduction (5 min)**
- The fraud challenge: $X billion in fraudulent life claims annually
- Traditional detection: Manual review, rule-based systems, slow response
- The opportunity: Real-time AI-powered fraud detection

**Life Claims Fraud Patterns (10 min)**
- Common fraud types:
  - Staged death claims
  - Beneficiary manipulation
  - Contestable period fraud
  - Exaggerated claims
- Red flags and indicators
- Data sources: Claims, policies, medical records, external data

**AI/ML Approach to Fraud Detection (15 min)**
- **Anomaly Detection**: Isolation forests, autoencoders for unusual patterns
- **Supervised Learning**: Historical fraud cases for model training
- **Feature Engineering**: Behavioral patterns, timing, relationships
- **Real-time Scoring**: API-based fraud risk assessment
- **Model Explainability**: Why a claim is flagged (SHAP/LIME)

**Implementation: From Detection to Action (10 min)**
- Real-time scoring API integration
- Automated case routing (high-risk → investigation, low-risk → fast-track)
- Investigator dashboards and workflows
- Continuous learning and model improvement

**Results & Business Impact (10 min)**
- Case study: 20-40% reduction in fraudulent payouts
- Cost savings: $1-3M annual savings per 1,000 fraudulent claims prevented
- Operational efficiency: 25-35% reduction in investigation workload
- ROI: 300-500% return on investment

**Q&A (10 min)**

### Key Messages
- "Real-time detection stops fraud before payment"
- "Explainable AI builds investigator confidence"
- "Every dollar prevented = direct P&L impact"
